How to Play Vision11

Introduction

Vision11 is India’s biggest Sports Game with an exponentially growing user base of over 2 crore sports fans. Create your team with real players from Cricket, Football, Basketball, and more!

Select A Match

Choose the match you want to play and show-off your skills. Pick your team and get ready for the action!

Select Your Playing11

Create your own team by selecting players within a defined virtual credit points.

Choose Your Captain & Vice Captain

Choose your captain & vice-captain to earn extra points. Captains earn 2x points, and vice-captains earn 1.5x points.

One League, Multiple Teams

Increase your winning chances by joining a “Multiple Entry” League with up to 30 different teams.

Become a Part of a League

Join a league or contest to compete with others, or invite your friends to join a private league.
